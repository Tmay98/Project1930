Have you ever bought more groceries than you needed because of a sale, only to throw out the extra? What if you could save even more money, and get rid of those extra groceries?

Introducing Grocer Together

It's web app that allows you to join local groups to bulk buy groceries together. This saves you money through bulk purchasing, and saves the environment through reducing food wasteage!

It also encourages a sense of community by meeting up with people and interacting with them. Perhaps share your favourite recipes, or try new ones that others recommend.

Sign up below, join a group, meet others, and start saving money and the environment!
